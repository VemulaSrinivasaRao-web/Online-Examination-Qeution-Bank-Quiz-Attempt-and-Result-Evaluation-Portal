Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems.
Algorithms are a set of rules to be followed in calculations.
Data Structures is a specialized format for organizing and storing data.
Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines.
Machine Learning is a branch of AI and computer science which focuses on the use of data and algorithms to imitate the way that humans learn.
Neural Networks are a series of algorithms that endeavor to recognize underlying relationships in a set of data.
Deep Learning is a subset of machine learning.
Software Engineering is the systematic application of engineering approaches to the development of software.
Database Management Systems are software systems used to manage databases.
Cloud Computing is the on-demand availability of computer system resources.
